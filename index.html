Hobbies of Declan Abers

Gymnastics

I have been doing gymnastics at OGA Oregon Gymnastics Academy for 13 years at the time of writing this. If I do qualify to nationals I will be one of 17 in the region. Our region (region 2) contains 5 states.

My favorite events ranked:

  3. Floor
  4. Vault
  5. Rings
  6. Pommel Horse
